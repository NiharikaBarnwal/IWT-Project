Online Exam

Select the subject to take the exam and read the instructions before starting.

Instructions to Candidates

  • The exam duration is 3 hours (180 minutes).
  • You must attempt the paper in the order of your choice. However, managing time efficiently is your responsibility.
  • Each question carries +1 mark for a correct answer.
  • No negative marking for incorrect answers.
  • Unanswered questions will not have any penalty.
  • Use the "Save and Next" button after answering each question.
  • You can use the "Mark for Review" option to revisit a question later.
  • Do not refresh the page or press the back button during the exam. This may lead to disqualification.
  • All calculations should be performed on the provided rough sheets.
  • You must submit the exam before the timer ends. If not, your answers will be automatically submitted.
  • Ensure that you have read and understood all questions before attempting them.

Note: Violation of instructions may result in disqualification. Please adhere to the rules strictly.
